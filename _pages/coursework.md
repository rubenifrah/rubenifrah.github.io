My Master's program at **Dauphine-PSL** is structured around a rigorous core of mathematical foundations followed by specialized research-oriented courses. Below is the breakdown of the curriculum.

## Semester 1: Foundations 

* **[Foundations of Machine Learning](https://www.di.ens.fr/%7Efbach/learning_theory_class_2025/index.html)** (Prof. [Francis Bach](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=6PJWcFEAAAAJ&hl=fr)) 
    * **Focus:** Learning theory from first principles.
    * **Topics:** Kernel methods (RKHS), convex optimization, generalization bounds, and statistical learning theory.
* **[Reinforcement Learning](https://www.di.ens.fr/olivier.cappe/Courses/IASD-RL/)** (Prof. [Olivier Cappé](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=erIXTCsAAAAJ&hl=fr)) 
    * **Focus:** Sequential decision making and the exploration-exploitation tradeoff.
    * **Topics:** MDPs, Bellman equations, temporal difference learning (SARSA, Q-learning), dynamic programming, Multi-armed Bandits (UCB, Thompson Sampling).
* **[Optimization for Machine Learning](https://www.lamsade.dauphine.fr/~croyer/teachOML.html)** (Prof. [Clément Royer](https://www.lamsade.dauphine.fr/~croyer/teach.html)) 
    * **Focus:** Algorithms for large-scale learning tasks.
    * **Topics:** Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD), variance reduction, automatic differentiation, and non-convex optimization landscapes.
* **Deep Learning for Image Analysis** (Prof. [Étienne Decencière](https://www.cmm.mines-paristech.fr/~decenciere/), [Thomas Walter](https://curie.fr/personne/thomas-walter) & [Santiago Velasco-Forero](https://people.cmm.minesparis.psl.eu/users/velasco/)
    * **Focus:** Modern computer vision architectures.
    * **Topics:** CNNs (ResNet, VGG), Semantic Segmentation (U-Net), variational auto-encoders, generative networks, vision transformers.
* **Large Language Models** (Prof. [Alexandre Allauzen](https://lamsade.dauphine.fr/~allauzen/))
    * **Focus:** NLP and sequence modeling.
    * **Topics:** The attention mechanism, transformer architectures, training strategies for LLMs and fine-tuning.
* **Data Acquisition & Processing** (Prof. [Pierre Senellart](https://pierre.senellart.com/)) 
    * **Focus:** The data pipeline.
    * **Topics:** Information acquisition, scraping the web for data extraction and management of structured data.
* **Data Science Lab** (Prof. [Alexandre Verine](https://www.alexverine.com/)) 
    * **Focus:** Hands-on application of SOTA techniques.
    * **Project:** Implementation of a novel data-science challenge requiring scientific rigor and engineering skills, such as advesarial attacks, GANs architecture
* **High-Dimensional Statistics** (Prof. [Vincent Rivoirard](https://www.ceremade.dauphine.fr/~rivoirar/)) 
    * **Focus:** Inference when parameters ($p$) exceed observations ($n$).
    * **Topics:** Lasso/Ridge penalization, variable selection (AIC/BIC), wavelet thresholding and False Discovery Rate (FDR) control.

## Semester 2: Advanced research topics

* **Mathematics of Deep Learning** (Prof. [Bruno Loureiro](https://bruno-loureiro.github.io/)) 
    * **Focus:** Theoretical understanding of why deep learning works.
    * **Topics:** Neural Tangent Kernel (NTK), the "lazy" limit, double descent phenomena, benign overfitting, random matrix theory.
* **Dimension Reduction & Manifold Learning** (Prof. Eddie Aamari) 
    * **Focus:** Geometry of high-dimensional data.
    * **Topics:** Manifold hypothesis, intrinsic dimension, spectral methods (ISOMAP, Laplacian Eigenmaps), UMAP.
* **Advanced Machine Learning** (Prof. [Yann Chevaleyre](https://www.lamsade.dauphine.fr/~ychevaleyre/)) 
    * **Focus:** Selected advanced topics.
    * **Topics:** Online learning algorithms, variational methods for generative models.
* **Non-Convex Inverse Problems** (Prof. [Irène Waldspurger](https://fr.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ir%C3%A8ne_Waldspurger))
    * **Focus:** Signal recovery from limited measurements.
    * **Topics:** Theoretical guarantees for non-convex optimization in phase retrieval, matrix completion.
* **Point Clouds & 3D Modeling** (Prof. [Jean-Emmanuel Deschaud](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=zR1n_4QAAAAJ&hl=fr))
    * **Focus:** 3D Deep Learning and geometric representations.
* **Bayesian Machine Learning** (Prof. Guillaume Kon Kam King) 
    * **Focus:** Uncertainty quantification and non-parametrics.
    * **Topics:** Gaussian processes, Dirichlet processes, Bayesian Neural Networks, Martingale posteriors.
* **Monte Carlo Search & Games** (Prof. [Tristan Cazenave](https://www.lamsade.dauphine.fr/~cazenave/index.php)) 
    * **Focus:** Planning and search in large state spaces.
    * **Topics:** MCTS (UCT, GRAVE), nested Monte Carlo, combining search with Deep Learning (AlphaZero style).
