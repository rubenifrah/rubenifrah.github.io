## Semester 2: Advanced research topics

### Geometric & Physical Learning
* [cite_start]**[Mathematics of Deep Learning](https://bruno-loureiro.github.io/)** (Prof. Bruno Loureiro) [cite: 25]
    * **Focus:** Theoretical understanding of why deep learning works.
    * **Topics:** Neural Tangent Kernel (NTK), the "Lazy" limit, double descent phenomena, benign overfitting, and random matrix theory.
* [cite_start]**[Dimension Reduction & Manifold Learning](https://www.lsta.upmc.fr/aamari/)** (Prof. Eddie Aamari) [cite: 20]
    * **Focus:** Geometry of high-dimensional data.
    * **Topics:** Manifold hypothesis, intrinsic dimension, Spectral methods (ISOMAP, Laplacian Eigenmaps), and UMAP.
* [cite_start]**[Non-Convex Inverse Problems](https://www.ceremade.dauphine.fr/~waldspurger/)** (Prof. Ir√®ne Waldspurger) [cite: 27]
    * **Focus:** Signal recovery from limited measurements.
    * **Topics:** Theoretical guarantees for non-convex optimization in phase retrieval and matrix completion.
* **[Point Clouds & 3D Modeling](https://cmm.mines-paristech.fr/users/jean-emmanuel-deschaud/)** (Prof. Jean-Emmanuel Deschaud)
    * **Focus:** 3D Deep Learning and geometric representations.

### Probabilistic & Generative Methods
* [cite_start]**[Bayesian Machine Learning](https://guillaumekkk.github.io/)** (Prof. Guillaume Kon Kam King) [cite: 18]
    * **Focus:** Uncertainty quantification and non-parametrics.
    * **Topics:** Gaussian Processes, Dirichlet Processes, Bayesian Neural Networks, and Martingale Posteriors.
* [cite_start]**[Monte Carlo Search & Games](https://www.lamsade.dauphine.fr/~cazenave/)** (Prof. Tristan Cazenave) [cite: 26]
    * **Focus:** Planning and search in large state spaces.
    * **Topics:** MCTS (UCT, GRAVE), Nested Monte Carlo, and combining Search with Deep Learning (AlphaZero style).
* [cite_start]**[Advanced Machine Learning](https://www.lamsade.dauphine.fr/~chevaleyre/)** (Prof. Yann Chevaleyre) [cite: 16]
    * **Focus:** Selected advanced topics.
    * **Topics:** Online learning algorithms, Variational methods for generative models.
* **[Machine Learning with Kernel Methods](https://julien.mairal.org/)** (Prof. Julien Mairal)
    * **Focus:** RKHS and sparse modeling.
