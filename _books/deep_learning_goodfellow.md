The "Deep Learning" book is intended to help students and practitioners enter the field of machine learning in general and deep learning in particular. It offers a comprehensive background in applied mathematics and machine learning theory, covering deep feedforward networks, regularization, optimization algorithms, convolutional networks, sequence modeling, and practical methodology.
