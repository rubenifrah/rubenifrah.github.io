This concise monograph provides a rigorous introduction to the fundamental algorithms of reinforcement learning. It focuses on the theoretical analysis of value-based and policy-based methods, offering insights into their convergence properties and sample complexity. It is an essential resource for understanding the mathematical foundations of RL algorithms.
